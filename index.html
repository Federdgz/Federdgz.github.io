Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our meals and services.

How does delivery work?

We deliver your meals in insulated, eco-friendly packaging to keep them fresh. Deliveries are made between 8am and 8pm on your selected delivery day. You'll receive a notification when your order is on its way, and you don't need to be home to receive it. Our packaging keeps meals fresh for up to 12 hours after delivery.

How long do meals stay fresh?

Our meals stay fresh in your refrigerator for up to 7 days from the delivery date. Each meal has a "best by" date clearly marked on the packaging. We recommend consuming them by this date for optimal taste and nutritional value.

Can I skip deliveries or cancel my subscription?

Yes, you have complete flexibility with your subscription. You can skip weeks, pause, or cancel your subscription anytime through your account dashboard. We just ask that changes be made at least 5 days before your scheduled delivery date.

What makes your packaging sustainable?

All our packaging is either compostable or recyclable. Our meal containers are made from plant-based materials that break down in home compost systems within 90 days. The insulation in our delivery boxes is made from recycled denim and is also compostable. Ice packs are non-toxic and the gel inside can be safely disposed of down the drain.

Do you accommodate allergies and dietary restrictions?

Yes, we offer meals that accommodate various dietary preferences including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, and nut-free options. All allergens are clearly labeled on each meal. While we take precautions to avoid cross-contamination, our meals are prepared in a facility that handles major allergens, so we cannot guarantee that meals are allergen-free.
